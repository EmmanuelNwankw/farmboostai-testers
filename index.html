1. About This Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the Modus FarmBoost AI mobile application ("the App"), developed and operated by Modus Technology ("we", "us", or "our"). The App is designed to support Nigerian farmers with AI-powered tools including crop disease scanning, satellite farm mapping, market price information, loan and credit management, farm financial records, and multilingual voice assistance.

By downloading, installing, or using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

2. Data We Collect

We collect the following categories of data to provide and improve the App's features:

Category Data Collected Purpose
Location Data GPS coordinates, farm boundary polygon points, device location Satellite farm mapping, land size calculation, agro-zone detection, planting calendar
Camera & Images Photos taken via camera or selected from gallery AI crop disease scanning, farm profile photo attachment
Voice & Audio Voice recordings captured during voice note or voice command sessions Speech-to-text transcription, voice command navigation, multilingual TTS responses
Financial Data Income and expense entries, loan application details, repayment schedules, monthly budget Farm ledger, loan tracker, repayment calendar, credit score calculation
Farm Profile Farm name, state, LGA, crop type, estimated yield, GPS boundary, land area (hectares) Farm verification report, Fintech loan vetting, planting calendar personalisation

Device Information Device model, OS version, app version, language preference Bug diagnostics, app compatibility, dialect auto-detection
Usage Data Screens visited, features used, session duration App improvement, feature prioritisation

Data You Provide Directly

When you use the App, you may voluntarily provide information such as your name, phone number, state, LGA, crop type, loan details, and income/expense records. This information is stored locally on your device and, where applicable, on our secure servers.

Data Collected Automatically

The App automatically collects certain technical data when you use it, including device identifiers, crash reports, and feature usage statistics. This data is anonymised and used solely for improving the App.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect for the following purposes:

  • Providing core features — crop scanning, satellite farm mapping, market prices, loan tools, ledger, planting calendar, and voice assistance.
  • Generating your Farm Credit Score — using farm activity, loan history, profile completeness, and financial records to calculate a composite creditworthiness score.
  • Producing Farm Verification Reports — generating GPS-verified reports for partner Fintech lenders (Moniepoint, NIRSAL MFB, Bank of Agriculture, etc.) when you choose to share them.
  • Sending push notifications — scan result summaries, loan status updates, repayment reminders, budget alerts, and planting date reminders.
  • Improving the App — analysing anonymised usage patterns to fix bugs and add new features.
  • Complying with legal obligations — where required by Nigerian law or regulatory bodies.

⚠️ We do not use your data for advertising, sell your data to third parties, or share your financial or health data without your explicit consent.

4. How We Share Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We may share data only in the following limited circumstances:

With Fintech Partners (With Your Consent)

When you tap "Send to Moniepoint", "Send to NIRSAL MFB", or similar buttons in the App, you are choosing to share your Farm Verification Report with that specific partner. This is always an explicit, user-initiated action. We do not share your data with any Fintech partner without your direct action.

With Service Providers

We use trusted third-party service providers to operate the App, including cloud hosting, AI inference, and push notification delivery. These providers are contractually bound to process your data only as instructed by us and may not use it for their own purposes.

For Legal Compliance

We may disclose your data if required to do so by law, court order, or a competent Nigerian regulatory authority, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Modus Technology, our users, or the public.

Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your data may be transferred to the acquiring entity, subject to the same privacy protections described in this policy.

6. Data Storage & Security

Local Storage

Most of your data — including farm profiles, scan history, financial records, and voice preferences — is stored locally on your device using encrypted AsyncStorage. This means your data remains on your phone even when you are offline.

Server Storage

Data that requires cloud processing (such as AI crop scan analysis and credit score calculation) is transmitted to our secure servers over HTTPS/TLS encryption. We do not store raw voice recordings on our servers beyond the time needed to process your request.

Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures including end-to-end encryption for data in transit, encrypted storage at rest, access controls, and regular security audits. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as necessary to provide the App's services or as required by law. Specifically:

  • Local device data — retained until you delete the App or clear the App's data in your device settings.
  • Server-side data — retained for a maximum of 24 months from your last active use of the App, after which it is permanently deleted.
  • Voice recordings — deleted from our servers immediately after transcription is complete (typically within seconds).
  • Anonymised usage analytics — may be retained indefinitely in aggregated, non-identifiable form.

You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at the address below.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for use by adults (18 years and older) and farmers operating agricultural businesses. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately and we will delete it promptly.

9. Your Rights

As a user of the App, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to Access — You may request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Correction — You may request that we correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to Deletion — You may request that we delete your personal data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Right to Withdraw Consent — Where processing is based on consent (e.g. location, microphone), you may withdraw consent at any time via your device settings.
  • Right to Data Portability — You may request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Right to Object — You may object to the processing of your data for certain purposes.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details in Section 12. We will respond to all requests within 30 days.
